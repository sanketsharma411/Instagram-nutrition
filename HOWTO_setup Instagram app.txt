1) Go to http://instagram.com/developer/
	and Register for an account
2) Select 'Register New Client' button
3) Fill in the details asked for	
	I unchecked both the check boxes
4) To get ACCESS_TOKEN:
	With your CLIENT_ID and REDIRECT-URI,
	go to https://api.instagram.com/oauth/authorize/?client_id=CLIENT-ID&redirect_uri=REDIRECT-URI&response_type=token
5) Click on Authorize
	In the Address bar you will have your access token in the form of
	http://instagram-project.appspot.com/?token=ACCESS_TOKEN
